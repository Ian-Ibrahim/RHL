About us

We are Renaissance Health Limited.

Established in 2018, Renaissance Health Limited is a strong industry leader distinguished by her core corporate values that places people at the center of everything. We have continued to provide our clients with trusted and quality products and services over the years. Renaissance Health Limited has a unique distribution and marketing network of more than 25 medical sales representatives and medical engineer. The company is managed by highly experience directors with diverse backgrounds and skill set.
